About me:

My name is João Dias and I was born in 2000 in Vila Nova de Gaia, Porto. My journey started back in 2006, when I first entered elementary school, as a child engineer and technology always kept myself hooked. While I was still ten it was already clear for me that I was going to pursue something related with engineer.

Fast-forward to today, I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Computer Engineer in Instituto Superior de Engenharia do Porto in September 2021 and I am now attending a master’s degree in Systems and Automations in the same institution. Currently I have still one year left (Sep. 2023 provisional end).

Alongside my academic duties I have some hobbies. One of them is practicing sports, from 10-18 years old I practiced canoeing, since then I only do gym. Another one is Home Automation, I recently started a project for automating my home and it has worked out quite well. Aside from those two I share great interest for sports, mainly Formula 1 and football.
